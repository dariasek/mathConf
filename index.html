We invite you to participate in the joint international Uzbek-Ukrainian Conference Modern problems of the theory of stochastic processes and their applications, October 10-11, 2023 (online). The aim of the Conference is to bring together knowledge from various fields of stochastic analysis, stochastic differential equations theory, and limit theorems for stochastic systems.

Main working language: English.
Form: online.
Form of the conference: plenary and sectional talks

Important dates:
August 20, 2023: the deadline for registration.
September 10, 2023 the deadline for acceptance of the abstracts of the talks.
October 10-11, 2023: Conference days
To participate in the conference, please fill in the registration form :

Register

Conference Proceedings:
The proceedings of the conference are planned to be published in the journal Theory of Stochastic Processes (Ukraine) after reviewing. Please, follow the requirements and use the template to type your paper (you can download these files from the journal Theory of Stochastic Processes (Ukraine), website http://tsp.imath.kiev.ua/).
Proceedings must contain original results not published in other publications.

Topics

  • Stochastic analysis
  • Numerical methods for stochastic systems
